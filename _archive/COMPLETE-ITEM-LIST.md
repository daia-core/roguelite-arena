## Hidden Mechanics

**Weapon Specialization Bonus:**
- All melee items = +20% damage
- All ranged items = +20% damage
- Mixed melee + ranged = no bonus

**Affinity System:**
- Each player gets 2 random tags at start (hidden)
- Shop items matching affinity = green ⚡SYNERGY glow
- Encourages tag-focused builds

**Free Reroll Trick:**
- Buy all 6 items in one wave = next reroll FREE
- Economic builds can abuse this with Midas Touch

**Recycling Strategy:**
- Early: buy defensive items (survive)
- Mid: recycle defensive (37.5% with Haggler)
- Late: buy offensive items (pivot to damage)
